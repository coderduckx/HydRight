About those who have made this Webapp



Hydrite was made during the 2015 Festival of Code, organised by Young Rewired State, and held at many places across the country. This particular webapp, was made at BBC Birmingham.




Our job as the game creators or developers
the programmers, artists, and whatnot, is that we have to kind of put ourselves in the user's shoes. We try to see what they're seeing, and then make it, and support what we think they might think.

Shigeru Miyamoto - Creator of the Legend of Zelda games
